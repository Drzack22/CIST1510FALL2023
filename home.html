This is Oooga-Booga Camp!

Whether youre a an excited kid who convinced their parents to bring you here, or a parent trying to get the kids out the house, Oooga-Booga camp is the perfect place for having fun in Georgia. With it just being a short hop to the swamps, millforge man-made rock climbing, and the Knarly Woodlands, here, the only “absolute-must” items on your list are the activities you enjoy most, experienced at your own pace! Theres plenty of fun for all just waiting to be discovered. So bring your curiosity, sense of wonder, A change of clothes (Trust us you'll need them!) and find out what our great Oooga-Booga camp has to offer.
