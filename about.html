Our Mission

Oooga-Booga camp is a non-profit organization that is run by an passionate group of friends that came together to make their vision a reality. What was their vision you may ask? To provide a place for the rising generation of the 21st century to experience the wonders of nature. Coryxkenshin, our founder, had these words to say about the creation of Oooga-Booga Camp.

“Well it's quite a funny story actually. You see, me and my best friend, or as you guys know him co-founder Dashie, were talking about the horror games that were coming out around this time. We had just finished a roblox horror game and were talking about the how it improved when it just hit me. 'what if we start a camp for the kids with a horror like name?'. It was history from there! ”
